# Re-Dream
I'm sure you have something that you've been wanting to make for a while, I know I do! This is your chance to finally work on that project that's been on the backburner!!

The purpose of Re-Dream is to work on something you're truly passionate about and we will provide you with the budget to make your dream game just as good as you've imagined it!

You can check out the [website here!](https://re-dream.vercel.app) and [rsvp here!](https://rsvp.soon.it/re-dream)

## You ship
- ANY VIDEO GAME YOU'RE PASSIONATE ABOUT!
- It can be a rhythm game, a platformer, or even a psychological horror!

## We Ship
- Every 10 hours you code you would get 500 chips added to your budget, you can use this budget to apply for grants to help out the development of your project!
- These grants can be used to purchase anything to help your projects development!
- If you need some art to make it look how you imagined, spend some of the budget to commission it!
- Need a library to make your life easier? Just get it with some of your budget!
-You want to publish your project to steam, well you can spend 1000 chips to do so!!
- The first 10 hours must be done before you will receive the first addition to your budget, but we will send you some stickers along with that first devlog!!
