Re-Dream gives you a real budget to build the game you've always wanted to make and you earn it by actually working on it.

Every 10 hours you code and log earns you 500 chips, which get added to your budget. The more time you put in, the more chips you stack up. Then you spend those chips on grants for whatever your project actually needs commissions, libraries, tools, or even a Steam publishing key.

It's simple: log hours > earn chips > spend chips on making your dream game as good as you've imagined.
